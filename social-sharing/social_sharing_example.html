What is Social Sharing?

Social sharing allows users to share your content on various social media platforms. It's essential for increasing your content's reach and engagement.

Open Graph Protocol Implementation

The Open Graph Protocol enables rich media sharing on social platforms. Here's how it's implemented:

<meta property="og:title" content="Your Page Title">
<meta property="og:description" content="Your page description">
<meta property="og:image" content="https://example.com/image.jpg">
<meta property="og:url" content="https://example.com/page">
<meta property="og:type" content="article">

Platform-Specific Sharing

Facebook Sharing

Facebook uses Open Graph Protocol extensively. Make sure to include:

  • og:title
  • og:description
  • og:image (recommended: 1200x630px)
  • og:url
  • og:type

Twitter Sharing

Twitter uses Twitter Cards for rich media sharing. Include:

  • twitter:card
  • twitter:title
  • twitter:description
  • twitter:image
  • twitter:site

LinkedIn Sharing

LinkedIn supports Open Graph Protocol and provides professional sharing:

  • og:title
  • og:description
  • og:image
  • og:url
  • article:author
  • article:published_time

Advanced Open Graph Features

Video Content

For video content, include additional meta tags:

<meta property="og:type" content="video">
<meta property="og:video" content="https://example.com/video.mp4">
<meta property="og:video:type" content="video/mp4">
<meta property="og:video:width" content="1280">
<meta property="og:video:height" content="720">
<meta property="og:video:duration" content="120">

Product Information

For e-commerce products, include pricing and availability:

<meta property="og:type" content="product">
<meta property="product:price:amount" content="29.99">
<meta property="product:price:currency" content="USD">
<meta property="product:availability" content="in stock">
<meta property="product:brand" content="My Brand">

Location Data

For location-based content:

<meta property="og:type" content="business.business">
<meta property="business:contact_data:street_address" content="123 Main St">
<meta property="business:contact_data:locality" content="New York">
<meta property="business:contact_data:region" content="NY">
<meta property="business:contact_data:postal_code" content="10001">
<meta property="business:contact_data:country_name" content="USA">
